Hi! Suyog Latake this side!

Sometimes I code, sometimes I design

I am Suyog Latake, a B.Tech 2nd-year student at IIT Gandhinagar, currently pursuing Chemical Engineering—yes, Chemical Engineering! But that hasn’t stopped me from exploring beyond boundaries and nurturing my passion for learning new things.

I am a passionate coder, constantly pushing myself beyond the curriculum. I have been exploring web, app, and game development—all in parallel! Learning and building across these domains keeps me engaged and fuels my curiosity.

I don’t just belong to the tech world—I equally belong to the designers’ world. Creativity is something I love bringing to life. I work with tools like Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, Illustrator, Canva, DaVinci Resolve, and Blender to craft visuals that speak.

I have been actively working as a Design and Tech Coordinator at EII (Entrepreneurship Initiative at IIT Gandhinagar) and the E-Summit IIT Gandhinagar for over a year now. I am leading a team of 20 designers and 10 tech enthusiasts, driving them toward making IIT Gandhinagar’s first-ever E-Summit an event to remember.
